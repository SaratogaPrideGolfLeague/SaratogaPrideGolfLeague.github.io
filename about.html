About Saratoga Pride Golf League

The Saratoga Pride Golf League emerged as a natural progression from the Saratoga Pride Festivities, which saw the resounding success of a spirited softball game. Inspired by this triumph, the league's founder, a lifelong golf enthusiast, sought to explore the possibility of engaging community members in additional physical activities beyond the annual softball event in Saratoga. The inception of the league was fueled by their passion for golf and the desire to connect with others who shared a similar interest in recreational pursuits.

As the league embarks on its inaugural season, it aims to establish itself as a welcoming and inclusive space. Designed to provide respite from the demands of the workweek, the Saratoga Pride Golf League offers participants the opportunity to unwind amidst the picturesque surroundings of 9-hole golf courses. With a focus on fostering a safe and affirming environment, the league invites individuals from diverse backgrounds to come together, enjoy the sport, and forge lasting connections within the LGBTQI+ community. Whether seasoned golfers or novices, members can relish the camaraderie and rejuvenation that accompanies an afternoon on the greens.
