League Scoring 101

Players may play off the tee they selected in the registration form.

Our Scoring System

We play a modified scoring system.

  • Double Bogey or worse = 0 points, 2 or more stokes than determined by your average.
  • Bogey = 1 point, 1 more stoke than determined by your average.
  • Par = 2 points, exact stokes as determined by your average.
  • Birdie = 4 points, 1 less stoke than determined by your average.
  • Eagle = 8 points, 2 less stokes than determined by your average.
  • Albatross = 16 points, 3 or less stokes than determined by your average.

Week 1 is play in week. We establish your average per week. Averages are cumulative.

(Example: Week 1 you score 4 points. Week 2 you score 8 points. Your second week score is +4 and your average rises to 6 for week 3).

We will play 4-person scrambles during the season. The 4-person scramble will be determined on a weekly basis.
